Welcome to the PDX integrator

Patient-derived xenograft mouse models (PDX) have emerged as an important oncology research platform to study tumor evolution, the mechanisms of drug response and resistance, and in the personalization of chemotherapeutic approaches for patients. PDX finder aim is to make all PDX models anywhere in the world findable and obtainable.
